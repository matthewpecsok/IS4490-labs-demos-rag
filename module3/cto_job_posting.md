# Job Posting: Chief Technology Officer

## Company
BrightPath Health Analytics

## Location
Salt Lake City, UT / Hybrid

## Position Type
Full-Time, Executive Leadership

## About BrightPath Health Analytics
BrightPath Health Analytics is a growth-stage healthcare technology company that builds data and AI-enabled tools for regional hospitals, outpatient clinics, and care management organizations. Our platform helps healthcare leaders identify operational bottlenecks, improve patient access, reduce administrative burden, and make better decisions using secure, compliant analytics.

We are seeking a Chief Technology Officer to lead our technology strategy as we scale from a successful regional product into a national healthcare analytics platform.

## Role Overview
The Chief Technology Officer will own the company’s technical vision, product architecture, engineering organization, security posture, and AI/data platform strategy. This person will work closely with the CEO, COO, Chief Product Officer, and Board of Directors to align technology investments with business growth.

The ideal candidate has experience leading engineering teams, scaling cloud-based SaaS platforms, managing security and compliance requirements, and translating business strategy into technical execution.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define and lead the company’s long-term technology strategy.
- Manage and mentor engineering, data engineering, DevOps, and security teams.
- Oversee architecture for a scalable, secure, cloud-based healthcare analytics platform.
- Partner with product leadership to prioritize roadmap items and technical investments.
- Ensure systems meet healthcare data security, privacy, and compliance expectations.
- Evaluate and guide responsible use of generative AI and machine learning features.
- Build processes for software quality, incident response, release management, and observability.
- Communicate technical priorities clearly to executive leadership and nontechnical stakeholders.
- Support enterprise sales conversations involving security, infrastructure, integrations, and data governance.
- Recruit senior technical talent and develop future engineering leaders.

## Required Qualifications
- 10+ years of technology leadership experience.
- 5+ years managing software engineering teams.
- Experience with SaaS platforms, cloud infrastructure, data platforms, and API-based systems.
- Strong understanding of cybersecurity, privacy, and regulated data environments.
- Ability to communicate effectively with executives, customers, investors, and technical teams.
- Experience making build-versus-buy decisions and managing technology budgets.

## Preferred Qualifications
- Healthcare technology, health analytics, or health data experience.
- Experience with HIPAA-regulated systems.
- Experience with AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
- Experience leading teams through rapid company growth.
- Familiarity with AI governance, machine learning operations, or generative AI products.
- Prior executive leadership experience at a venture-backed or growth-stage company.

## Compensation
$210,000–$280,000 base salary, plus bonus eligibility and equity.

## Application Materials
Applicants should submit a resume and a brief cover letter explaining their experience leading technology teams and scaling software platforms.
